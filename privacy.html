👋 About This Policy

Welcome to Pregnancy Tracker, developed by Orbay Kahraman. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

By using Pregnancy Tracker, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📋 Information We Collect

Pregnancy Tracker collects the following data that you voluntarily provide:

  • Pregnancy information: Due date, last menstrual period date, baby name
  • Health data: Weight logs, mood entries, feeding logs (for postpartum tracking)
  • Cycle data: Menstrual cycle dates, cycle length, period duration
  • Photos: Baby photos you choose to add within the app
  • Purchase information: In-app purchase status (managed by Apple and RevenueCat)

We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials.

☁️ iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud, your app data is synced across your Apple devices using Apple CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

💳 In-App Purchases

Pregnancy Tracker offers optional premium subscriptions and one-time purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple App Store and managed by RevenueCat. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data to manage subscription status.

For RevenueCat's privacy practices, visit: revenuecat.com/privacy

We do not store your payment or credit card information.

📸 Camera & Photo Library

The app may request access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to add baby photos within the app. Photos are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud. We do not upload your photos to any external server.

🔔 Notifications

With your permission, Pregnancy Tracker may send local notifications to remind you of contraction timers or weekly milestone updates. These notifications are generated on-device and do not involve any external server.

🤝 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple CloudKit – Data sync across devices
  • RevenueCat – Subscription and purchase management

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or sell your data to third parties.

👶 Children's Privacy

Pregnancy Tracker is designed for use by adults (expectant mothers and their partners). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

🔒 Data Security

All your data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not operate external servers that store your personal health information. Your data is protected by your device's security and Apple's iCloud encryption.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from your device settings. Since we do not store your data on external servers, uninstalling the app removes all locally stored information.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
